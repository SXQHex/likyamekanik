## Basic Design Criteria 

Three main parameters are decisive in fire installation design: 

- Flow 
- Pressure 
- Risk class 

</Section> 

<RegulationBox
  title="Binaların Yangından Korunması Hakkında Yönetmelik"
  article="Madde 92"
> 
In sprinkler system design, minimum flow rate and pressure must be provided according to risk class. 
</RegulationBox> 

<Section title="Debi Hesabı"> 

The design flow rate is calculated as follows: 

<InlineMath formula="Q = n × K × √P" /> 

Here: 

- n → Number of sprinklers turned on 
- K → Nozzle coefficient 
- P → Pressure 

</Section>

## Result 

Regulation compliance alone is not sufficient in fire installation design. Hydraulic calculation, zoning and risk analysis should be handled together.
